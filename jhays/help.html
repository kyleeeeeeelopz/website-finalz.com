Help - T-Shirt Products

Placing an Order

If you're having trouble placing an order, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account or create a new account if you're a new customer.
  2. Browse our selection of t-shirts and choose the ones you want to purchase.
  3. Add the selected items to your shopping cart.
  4. Proceed to checkout and enter your shipping and payment information.
  5. Review your order and confirm the purchase.
Shipping and Delivery

Here's what you need to know about shipping and delivery:

  • We offer standard and expedited shipping options.
  • Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected.
  • You can track your order using the tracking information provided.
  • If you have any questions about your shipment, please contact our customer support team.
Returns and Exchanges

If you need to return or exchange an item, please follow our return policy:

  1. Contact our customer support team to initiate a return or exchange process.
  2. Make sure the item is in its original condition with tags attached.
  3. Ship the item back to us within the specified return period.
  4. Once we receive the item, we will process your refund or exchange.
